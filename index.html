SKYROGUE
A Roguelike Card Adventure

How to Play

  1. A card is offered face-up — Swap it into yer grid or Toss it
  2. Toss = discard it (limited!) and flip a face-down card
  3. 3 in a row/column? Same character, straight (3-4-5), or same suit (⚔️🔮💀) → Eliminated!

Goal: keep yer score at or below the target.
